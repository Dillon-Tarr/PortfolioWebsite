Budget Assistant

This project is the project I created as my capstone during devCodeCamp's Web Development course. I used the MERN stack, plus Redux.

The purpose of the app is to help people organize their planned income and outgo (expenses), see reminders of upcoming outgo, and view helpful information based on the values entered.

RPSLS
