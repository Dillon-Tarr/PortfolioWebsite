RPSLS

From the creative minds of Sam Kass and Karen Bryla comes a two player game in the form of a spin-off of "Rock Paper Scissors", called "Rock Paper Scissors Spock Lizard".

It was popularized by the writers of The Big Bang Theory as "Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock".

Click or tap above to download my browser version of this game and play it against the computer or against a friend.
